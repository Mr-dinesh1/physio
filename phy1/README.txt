## Features

- Home: Provides an overview of the clinic, its mission, and the services offered.
- About: Presents information about the clinic's history, team of physiotherapists, and their qualifications.
- Services: Describes the different physiotherapy services provided, including specialized treatments.
- Appointments: Allows patients to request appointments online by filling out a form or using a scheduling tool.
- FAQs: Answers frequently asked questions about physiotherapy, the clinic, and the appointment process.
- Contact: Provides contact information, including phone number, email address, and a contact form for inquiries.
- Responsive Design: Ensures the website adapts to different screen sizes and devices for a seamless browsing experience.
